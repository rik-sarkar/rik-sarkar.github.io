Freelance Work


Daybreaker

A company known as Daybreaker, requested me to develop a social media graphic for their event “A JOY SUPREME: DANCING TO MOTOWN MAGIC”. I illustrated old time Motown imagery and selected similar aesthetic typography for the visuals.


MedicZero

In an effort to educate high school students about medical emergencies, I was contracted to animate graphics over a video discussing and explaining the specificities behind a stroke and what to do in response to somebody experiencing a stroke.

Rutgers Undergraduate Law Review (RUULR)

As a part of the revamp of the Rutgers Undergraduate Law Review, the president contacted me to design a logo for the club that inluded Lady Justice. He wanted me to incorporate the Rutgers shield of the “Scarlet Knight” with the symbol of law and justice.
