Institute for
Domestic and International Affairs (IDIA)


The Institute for Domestic and International Affairs is a non profit corporation dedicated towards educating, empowering, and engaging students from different high schools in a unique experience. This organization develops and builds a simulated United Nation conferences in different areas and brings in high schools from different parts of the country, and is administered as a program called Model United Nations (MUN).


Rutgers Model United Nations (RUMUN)

Model UN takes place in different areas on the East Coast and in 2019, IDIA organized this conference that took place at Rutgers and was known as “RUMUN” . RUMUN 2019's theme was "Dichotomy of Partnerships", and I was tasked to help create a logo that would not only encapsulate that theme, but also bring that theme alive during the conference. This logo would be used interchangeably through different mediums, from t-shirts to cups, and any other forms of merchandising/branding.
