Viaje

Starter Posts

Viaje is a traveling app that hired me to develop a marketing plan, social media graphics, and animated visuals to begin promotion of their product. After developing a marketing plan to kickstart Viaje’s social media campaign, I created three posts specifically to make up Viaje’s overall Instagram palette and grid, while catering the posts’ content to explaining the app in different ways. These posts are intended to be swipeables, and required me to animate parts of the brand identity, while using pastel colors to help set the tone of the social media presence.

Oh, The Places You’ll Go! Posts

The client requested a type of photo manipulation of places around the world to help instill the traveling mindset in our audience. As a result, I combined images from trips to these areas with my hand-animated bullet journal styled illustrations.

Travelling Place-List/Playlist

To resonate with Gen Z, I took elements of Apple Music/Spotify/and other popular music streaming apps, and used them as a template to give audiences recommendations of places to visit. The client named this series “Place-lists” as an intended pun on the word “Playlists”. This series of posts was intended to deliver the app's mission of making travel easier in a more unique and familiar format to the audience.

Travelling Tips: Do’s/Donts in Miami

As a part of our effort to bring people into the mindset of travelling, I illustrated and animated tips to keep in mind when visiting Miami. Taking a list of Do’s And Don’ts from the client, and visualizing it into this 1 minute sequence.
